BENIFITS

One serving of Sambar gives 139 calories. Out of which carbohydrates comprise 66 calories,
proteins account for 24 calories and remaining calories come from fat which is 50 calories.
Weight Loss benefits
Since the stew is rich in proteins, fibres and is easily digestible, and sambar makes for a
delicious weight loss friendly food. To maximise on the weight loss benefits,you can increase
the quantity of vegetables in the stew and decrease the amount of salt.
This popular South-Indian dish not only pleases our taste buds but also does wonders for our health.
It doesn't harm our body even if we include it in our daily food and is considered as the best
healthy option for vegetarians as it is a great source of proteins.
