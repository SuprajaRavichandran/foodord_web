ARE YOU IN FIRST STAGE OF OBESITY?

What to Eat at this stage?

Choose minimally processed, whole foods:
1.Whole grains (whole wheat, steel cut oats, brown rice, quinoa)
2.Vegetables (a colorful variety-not potatoes)
3.Whole fruits (not fruit juices)
4.Nuts, seeds, beans, and other healthful sources of protein (fish and poultry)
5.Plant oils (olive and other vegetable oils)
6.Drink water or other beverages that are naturally calorie-free.

Limit these foods and drinks:

1.Sugar-sweetened beverages (soda, fruit drinks, sports drinks)
2.Fruit juice (no more than a small amount per day)
3.Refined grains(white bread, white rice, white pasta) and sweets
4.Potatoes (baked or fried)
5.Red meat (beef, pork, lamb) and processed meats (salami, ham, bacon, sausage)
6.Other highly processed foods, such as fast food
