Human and animal research has demonstrated that curcumin, the main compound in turmeric, may significantly improve markers of mental decline and reduce the risk of developing Alzheimer's disease May improve feelings of fullness. Curry powder contains coriander and cumin, two spices that test-tube studies have shown to have antifungal and antibacterial effects.
